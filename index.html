about.

Hello, my name is Nicholas. I'm a full-stack Web Developer based in Miami.

My sense of curiosity and love of exploration led me to work in the yachting industry, where I had the chance to travel to some amazing places, from the Caribbean to Europe and the United States. In that role, I learned how to work effectively in a team and how to think on my feet under pressure, skills that I've found to be valuable in all areas of life.

Web development in particular captured my imagination, and I was eager to learn as much as I could about this exciting field.
That's why I enrolled in the Software Engineer Immersive course at General Assembly. This program has allowed me to hone my technical skills and explore my creative interests in a supportive and challenging environment. Now, I'm ready to take the next step and find a role as a developer where I can continue to grow and thrive as part of a dynamic and friendly team.

projects.

Hustle Seeker

One week solo full-stack project built using Python, Django, and PostgreSQL on the back-end and React, JavaScript, Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS/SASS on the front-end.

Hustle Seeker is a web-app where you can search for jobs, apply to jobs, post reviews and also post your own jobs.

Europe Travel Guide

3-person full-stack project over the course of one and a half weeks. Using React, JS, Express, Node.js, MongoDB/Mongoose, Bootstrap, and SASS to develop a website and a CRUD API for traveling around Europe. My duties included creating the database models, implementing add/delete reviews in the back-end, posting reviews with images and displaying the stars on the front-end.

Europe Travel Guide is a web-app that allows the user to check information about their next destination. They can see what is to do, where to eat and where to sleep.

Bob's Burgers

Pair programming project over the course of 1.5 days developing a website using a public API, using React, JS, HTML, CSS/SCSS, Bootstrap.

Junior Star

One week, Solo, Front-end project using HTML, CSS and Vanilla JavaScript.

Junior Start is a game based on the game Frogger. You must dribble the ball across the pitch, in between the defenders, and score goals to help your team win the match. The player must score all goals in under 15 seconds in order to win the game. After each goal, the defenders will pay much more attention to you and try to block you faster.
